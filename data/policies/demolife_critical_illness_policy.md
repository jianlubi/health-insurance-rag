# SECTION 0 – POLICY SCHEDULE (Sample)

This Policy Schedule forms part of the Policy and specifies the coverage applicable to the Insured Person. Where the Policy Schedule conflicts with general provisions, the Policy Schedule shall prevail.

**Sample Details**

- Effective Date: January 1, 2026  
- Issue Age: 42  
- Benefit Amount: $100,000  
- Premium Mode: Monthly  
- Smoking Status: Non-Smoker  
- Riders Selected: Early-Stage Cancer Rider  

All benefits and premiums are subject to underwriting approval and the terms of this Policy.

---

# SECTION 1 – DEFINITIONS

For the purposes of this Policy:

**1.1 Policyholder**  
The individual who owns the Policy and is responsible for premium payments.

**1.2 Insured Person**  
The individual whose health is covered under this Policy.

**1.3 Effective Date**  
The date coverage begins as shown in the Policy Schedule.

**1.4 Waiting Period**  
A period of ninety (90) days from the Effective Date during which no benefits are payable for Covered Critical Illness.

**1.5 Survival Period**  
The Insured Person must survive for at least thirty (30) days following the date of diagnosis. No benefit is payable if the Insured Person dies during the Survival Period.

**1.6 Pre-Existing Condition**  
Any illness, symptom, medical consultation, diagnosis, or treatment occurring within twenty-four (24) months prior to the Effective Date that was not fully disclosed during application.

**1.7 Diagnosis**  
A diagnosis confirmed by a licensed specialist and supported by clinical, radiological, or pathological evidence acceptable to DemoLife Insurance.

**1.8 Covered Critical Illness**  
An illness listed in Section 4 that meets the medical criteria and is diagnosed after the Waiting Period.

---

# SECTION 2 – ELIGIBILITY AND ISSUE CONDITIONS

**2.1 Issue Age**  
Applicants must be between ages 18 and 65 at the time of issue.

**2.2 Underwriting**  
Coverage is subject to medical underwriting and full disclosure of health history.

**2.3 Material Misrepresentation**

If material information is omitted or misrepresented:

- 2.3.1 The Policy may be voided within the first two policy years; or  
- 2.3.2 Premiums or benefits may be adjusted based on corrected information.

**2.4 Coverage Termination Age**

Coverage terminates at age 75 unless otherwise specified in the Policy Schedule.

---

# SECTION 3 – PREMIUM PROVISIONS

**3.1 Premium Determination**

Premiums are based on:

- Age at issue  
- Smoking status  
- Benefit Amount  
- Selected riders  

**3.2 Payment Terms**

- Premiums are payable monthly or annually  
- A thirty (30) day Grace Period applies after each due date  
- Coverage continues during the Grace Period  

**3.3 Lapse**

If premium is not received within the Grace Period, coverage will lapse and no benefits will be payable.

**3.4 Reinstatement**

A lapsed Policy may be reinstated within twelve (12) months subject to:

- Payment of overdue premiums  
- Evidence of insurability  
- Approval by DemoLife Insurance  

**3.5 New Waiting Period**

A new Waiting Period applies following reinstatement (see Section 1.4).

---

# SECTION 4 – COVERED CRITICAL ILLNESSES

Benefits are payable upon first diagnosis of a Covered Critical Illness, including:

- Major Cancer (excluding carcinoma in situ unless Early-Stage Rider applies)  
- Heart Attack with confirmed myocardial infarction  
- Stroke resulting in permanent neurological deficit  
- Major Organ Failure  
- End-Stage Renal Disease  
- Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery  

Diagnosis must:

- Occur after the Waiting Period; and  
- Meet the Survival Period requirement (see Section 1.5).

---

# SECTION 5 – BENEFITS AND LIMITATIONS

**5.1 Full Benefit**

Upon confirmed diagnosis and completion of the Survival Period:

- 100% of the Benefit Amount is payable as a lump sum.

**5.2 Policy Termination After Payment**

Upon payment of the full Benefit Amount, this Policy terminates and no further benefits are payable (see Section 9).

**5.3 Early-Stage Cancer Rider**

If selected:

- 25% of the Benefit Amount is payable for eligible early-stage cancers  
- The remaining coverage is reduced by the amount paid  

**5.4 Multiple Claims**

Only one full Critical Illness benefit is payable per Policy.

**5.5 Recurrence**

No benefit is payable for recurrence of the same illness.

---

# SECTION 6 – EXCLUSIONS

No benefit will be payable for any condition arising from:

- 6.1 Pre-existing conditions not disclosed (see Section 1.6)  
- 6.2 Illness diagnosed during the Waiting Period  
- 6.3 Self-inflicted injury or attempted suicide  
- 6.4 Substance abuse or intoxication  
- 6.5 Participation in criminal activity  
- 6.6 War, terrorism, or civil unrest  
- 6.7 Hazardous activities not disclosed at application  

---

# SECTION 7 – CLAIM PROCESS

To submit a claim, the Policyholder must provide:

- 7.1 Completed claim form  
- 7.2 Physician’s diagnosis report  
- 7.3 Supporting medical records  

**7.4 Additional Requirements**

DemoLife Insurance may:

- Request additional documentation  
- Require an independent medical examination  
- Review medical history related to the condition  

**7.5 Processing Time**

Claims will be processed within thirty (30) business days after receipt of complete documentation.

---

# SECTION 8 – CLAIM REVIEW AND FRAUD

**8.1 Appeals**

If a claim is denied, the Policyholder may submit additional information within sixty (60) days.

**8.2 Secondary Review**

DemoLife Insurance will conduct a secondary review upon receipt of new information.

**8.3 Fraud**

If fraud or intentional misrepresentation is suspected:

- Claim processing may be suspended  
- The Policy may be voided if fraud is confirmed (see Section 9)

---

# SECTION 9 – TERMINATION

Coverage terminates upon the earliest of:

- 9.1 Payment of full Benefit Amount  
- 9.2 Non-payment beyond the Grace Period  
- 9.3 Written cancellation by the Policyholder  
- 9.4 Reaching age 75  
- 9.5 Policy voidance due to misrepresentation or fraud
